About

Welcome! I am Doris (Fengxinyi) Zhao, a PhD candidate in accounting at Scheller College of Business, Georgia Institute of Technology (expected 2027). My research interests lie in management accounting and control, ethical decision-making, and AI in accounting.

I hold a Master of Accounting Science (Tax and Data Analytics) and a Bachelor of Science in Accountancy from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.
